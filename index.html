ABOUT AS


Hotel Sariska Inn is a sprawling and well-maintained property with green lawns and spacious rooms, situated on the Alwar-Jaipur Highway. Tourist spots close to the hotel include Shiv Mandir, Hanuman Mandir, Siliserh lake for boating and Aravalli Garden. The hotel is surrounded by forested hills and boasts of well-maintained large lawns. It has a lively in-house restaurant with a trendy false ceiling. The hotel is wheelchair accessible as well. The rooms are very spacious, well-lit and tastefully decorated. Each room has a window seat. All rooms in the hotel come with AC, TV, and free Wi-Fi. The bathrooms have trendy fixtures. Ample parking space, CCTV security, power backup and laundry services are offered. The hotel is located at a driving distance from eateries or restaurants like The Peppers, Navratan, Akash and Foji Raj Dhaba.
